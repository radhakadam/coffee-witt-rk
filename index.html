about us

what makes our coffee special?

We all love a good shot of java to start the morning off right… but for the dedicated enthusiasts, coffee is more than just a drink: it’s an obsession. And for those true-brew coffee lovers, there is specialty coffee. But just what is specialty coffee, exactly? Well, it’s not just your average cup of joe.

Specialty coffee is more than the work of one person. It comes from the combined efforts of everyone along the chain of production. From farmers and harvesters to roasters and brewers, specialty coffee is the result of passion, precision, and professionalism. All this work goes into producing the very finest cups of coffee, and it’s this loving attention to detail that makes specialty coffee so, well, special!
